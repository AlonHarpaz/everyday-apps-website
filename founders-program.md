# Everyday Apps - Founders Program

## Company Tagline
Building the essential app suite for monday.com teams

## Company Description
We're building a comprehensive suite of productivity apps for the monday.com ecosystem. Starting with Everyday Import - a tool that lets users import data from CSV, Excel, and Google Sheets in seconds - we're expanding to forms, email automation, e-signatures, dashboards, and integrations. Our apps are built with enterprise security standards (SOC 2, network isolation) and deployed across multiple regions. We're a certified monday.com partner focused on solving the everyday pain points that teams face.
